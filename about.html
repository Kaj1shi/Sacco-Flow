Our Story

From Paper Files to Digital Confidence

We started SaccoFlow after seeing first-hand how hard SACCO teams work — and how much time they lose to manual processes, missing records, and tools that don’t fit the Ugandan context.

Built on the Ground in Uganda

What started this journey was recognizing that SACCOs are critical to Uganda's financial inclusion, yet many struggle with manual systems that limit growth and accountability.

We felt compelled to build a technology solution that empowers them to operate with confidence, security, and scale. A technology built specifically for the Ugandan cooperative finance ecosystem.

Designed for Real People, Not Just Data

We believe the best SACCO software should feel simple, trustworthy, and supportive — not intimidating. That’s why SaccoFlow focuses on clear dashboards, mobile access for members, and reporting that board members can understand at a glance.

Behind every transaction is a member saving for school fees, building a home, or starting a business. Our mission is to help SACCOs serve those members with excellence.
