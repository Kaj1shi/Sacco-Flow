FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. If you don't find yours here, just reach out.

Is our SACCO data secure on SaccoFlow? +

Yes. SaccoFlow uses bank-grade encryption, daily automated backups, and role-based access controls to ensure your data is always safe. Your data is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with 99.9% uptime.

How long does onboarding take? +

Most SACCOs are fully onboarded and operational within 3–7 days. Our team handles data migration, staff training, and configuration so you can hit the ground running with zero disruption to your operations.

Do members need smartphones to use SaccoFlow? +

No. While SaccoFlow has a full mobile portal for smartphone users, members can also receive updates via SMS on any phone. SaccoFlow works for both urban and rural members regardless of device type.

Is training provided for our staff? +

Absolutely. All plans include comprehensive staff training — online for Basic and Growth plans, and on-site training for Enterprise customers. We also provide ongoing support via WhatsApp, phone, and email.

Can SaccoFlow handle multiple branches? +

Yes. The Enterprise plan is designed for SACCOs with multiple branches, allowing consolidated reporting, branch-level access controls, and centralized member management across all your locations.

What happens if I need to cancel? +

You can cancel anytime. We'll provide a full export of all your data in standard formats (Excel/PDF) so you're never locked in. We're confident you won't want to leave once you see the results.
